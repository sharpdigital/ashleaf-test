Bronze leaf subscription

We’re launching a new instalment payment plan/monthly subscription service this year. It will take shape in the form of a monthly recurring payment of £10 a month for 12 months and will be limited to 100 people in the first year. It will be open to subscribers internationally and the monthly cost will include shipping worldwide.

We’re going to participate in the 100 Day Project this year. It is a global art project taking place between April and July. Creatives participate by committing to making or doing something for 100 continuous days. Our plan is to collect each day a single leaf from a different location that will be cast it in bronze. In the end, we will have a 100 unique bronze leaves coming from all over the UK where we travelled throughout this time. On the reverse of each bronze leaf the name of the leaf, the location and the date will be engraved. This collection will be a map and diary of 100 days from our art practice represented by leaves.

The 100 bronze leaves that will be the result of this project, will be the ones our subscribers can choose from in Autumn when we complete the collection. All bronze leaves will be dispatched by the 1st of December to arrive before Christmas.

We will offer this new service primarily to make our work more accessible and affordable for those who would otherwise be unable to acquire our work. This would help spread the cost of a unique bronze leaf sculpture throughout the year. Meanwhile, it would also support the continuation of our small art practice with regular subscribers we can rely on each month.
