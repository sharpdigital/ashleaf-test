The reality of an Ashleaf sculpture in your hand far surpasses what any photograph of it can portray. The way that the delicate and fragile beauty of a real leaf gets captured is extraordinary.

Mark, Hertfordshire

Our bronze sculptures preserve the most characteristic features of leaves in solid bronze. The tactility and hyperrealistic qualities in three dimensions embodied in our work push the boundaries of contemporary art, sculpture, traditional crafts and botanical art.

We aim to raise questions about the potential to reach beyond traditional classifications of art, crafts and science by creating a platform where a multidisciplinary practice can thrive. We invite exchange of perceptions and sharing knowledge about the natural world with the medium of leaves.
